Ba Mukisa
Blessed Are They that Do

Doh is A 
1. Wulir(a) ebigambo bya Yesu, Bya bulamu bya mazima;
Gw(e) ataty(a), atasaba, Wulira leero,
Ba mukis(a) abakwata ebyo.

Ba mukis(a) abakwat(a) amateeka,
Ba mukisa- Ba mukisa;
Ba mukis(a) abakwat(a) amateeka,
Ba mukisa, Nga besiimye!

2. Okuwulira nga tokola, (E)bisuubizo bye tofuna;
Okubiwulira kyokka tekiyamba,
Ba mukis(a) abakola ebyo.

3. Baliyingira mu kibuga, Teri kibi teri nnaku;
Nga batukuziddw(a) emirembe gyonna,
Balye ku muti (o)gwobulamu.
