Fuga Obulamu Bwange
Live Out Thy Life Within Me
An
4
4
Samuel Wesley
Doh is Eb 
1. Fug(a) obulamu bwange, Ggwe Yesu Kabaka,
Ggwe amagezi gange, Mu byonn(a) ebinnema;
Yesu, beeranga mu nze, Mu byonna bye nkola,
Mbeere ng(a) endabirwamu, Ndage (e)kitiibwa kyo.

2. Ka mbeere yekaalu yo, Omutali kibi,
Ekitiibwa kyo kyonna, Nga kyeraga mu nze;
Eby'ensi ka mbiveeko, Okuva kaakano
Mbeere muddu wo ddala, Nkole by'ondagira.

3. Ayi Yesu, nze nzenna, Ka mbeeranga wuwo,
Nkozesa by'oyagala, Sikyali ku bwange;
Bulijjo nettanira (O)kulu'ngamizibwa,
N'okulongoosebwa Ggwe, Mu mutima gwange.

4. Naye kannewombeeke, Mu biseera byonna,
Nga nnind(a) okuteesa kwo, Ggwe nga bw'oyagala;
Fug(a) obulamu bwange, Yesu Ggwe Kabaka,
Ggwe amagezi gange, Mu byonna bye nkola.
