CH 332
Ayi Yesu Amaaso Gange
Jesus, These Eyes Have Never Seen
4
4
Thomas Tallis
Doh is D 
1. Ayi Yesu amaaso gange, Tegakulaganga;
(E)kizikiza bwe kibikka, Ssiraba maaso go

2. Ssikulaba ssiwulira, Ddoboozi lyo Yesu!
Nga obeera kumpi nange, Mu buli kusaba.

3. Ensi terinaayo kifo, Kya ssanyu nga ekyo;
(E)kifo we ndabaganira, Ne Yesu munnange.

4. Ng(a) ebirooto eby'essanyu, Ebijja mu tulo,
Embala ye ngirowoza, Ne nfun(a) okwesiima.
