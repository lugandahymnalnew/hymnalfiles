Mukama Kino Kyensaba
One Thing I of the Lord Desire

Fred H Byshe
Doh is Bb 
1. Mukama kino kye nsaba, Ebyonoono byange byonna;-
Obinaaze n'amazzi go; Ontukuze, ontukuze.

Nnaaza munda ne kungulu, Onnongoose n'omuliro;
Ebibi byange biggwewo, Ontukuze, ontukuze.

2. Bw'onompa okwolesebwa, Ononsanyusa omwoyo,-
Nay(e) omutim(a) omulungi, Ggwe nneegomba, gwe nneegomba.

3. Bw'ononnongoos(a) omutima, Kye kirabo ekisinga,-
Ne gufaanana ng'ogugwo, (O)mutukuvu, (o)mutukuvu.

4. (E)kkubo ebbi ka ndireke, Mu birowoozo ebibi,-
Naye mu kufuba kwange, Sisobola kwetukuza.
