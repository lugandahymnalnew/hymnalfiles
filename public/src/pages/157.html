Kabaka Ali Ku Luggi
The Coming King
An
4
4
E E Belden
Doh is G 
1. Kabaka ali ku luggi, Eyafa ku lwa (a)bonoonyi
Ajjidde mu kitiibwa kye, Atutwale eka.

Ku luggi, Ku luggi; Yesu ali kumpi ku luggi,
Yesu ajja, Tumulinde, Ali kumpi ku luggi.

2. (O)bubonero bw'okuddakwe, Butuukirira mangu nyo;
Mangu nnyo tulimulaba, Lw'alidda n'amaanyi.

3. Temunoonya ssanyu lya nsi, N'ekitiibwa n'emirembe,
Yesu yaggyawo ekibi, Tuwummule mu Ye.

4. Nga tutuuse mu nsi empya, Abatukuvu be bonna,
Tulitikkirwa engule, Mu maaso ga Yesu.
