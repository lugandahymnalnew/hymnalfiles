CH 433
Omusingi Gw'ekkanisa
The Church Has One Foundation
4
4
Samuel S Wesley
Doh is Eb 
1. (O)musingi gw'ekkanisa, Ye Yesu Mukama;
Kye kitonde ky(e) ekiggya, Mu mazzi n'Enjiri;
Ye yamuggya mu ggulu, Eb(e) Omugole we;
(O)musaayi gwagigula, Yafa ebe nnamu.

2. Eri mu buli ggwanga, Naye eri emu;
Mukama, (o)kukkiriza, (o)kubatiza kumu;
(E)rinnya limu lyesinza, Erya emmer(e) emu,
Erina essuubi limu, Okudda kwa Yesu.

3. Newakubadd(e) enyoomwa, Era n’esaasirwa;
Abalabe baayo ne bwebanagiyuuza;
Abakkiriza baayo, Tebajjulukuke;
Er(a) obudde tebuulwe, Bayimb(e) olw’essanyu.

4. Mu kubonaabon(a) ennyo, Mu kufuba kwayo;
Esuubir(a) emirembe, Egy’olubeerera;
(O)kutuusa lweriraba Ekyasuubizibwa,
Olw(o) erib(a) ewangudde, Er(a) eriwummula.
