CH 623
Wonya Bonna
Rescue the Perishing
4
4
George A Minor
Doh is Bb 
1. Wonya bonna ab(o) abali mu kibi,
Bakwakule okuva mu kufa;
Saasira abantu abalumw(a) ennyo,
Balag(e) obulokozi bwa Yesu.

Leet(a) abantu bonna, Abali mu kufa,
Bawone olw'ekisa kya Yesu.

2. Newankubadde nga bo bamunyooma,
Ye yetaaga okubalokola.
Abayita bonna n'okwagala kwe,
Bwe bakkiriza anaasonyiwa.

3. Ab(o) abayonoonebwa omulabe,
Mukama ajja kubazza buggya;
Olwokwagala kwe, era n'ekisa,
Anawoonya buli (e)kyonoonese.

4. Fubanga ku lw'abo bonn(a) abaabula,
Mukama anakuwa amaanyi.
Bakomyewo bangi mu kkubo (e)ddungi;
Bateegez(e) eyafa ku lw'ababi.
