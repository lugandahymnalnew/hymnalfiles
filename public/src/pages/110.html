Waliwo Oluggi Oluggule
There is a Gate That Stands Ajar
An
4

S J Vail
Doh is C 
1. Waliwo (o)luggi (o)luggule, Omwo mwe ndabira nze,
Okwagala kwa Mukama, Okwamunfiiriza nze. - - -

Nga kwagala kunene nnyo,
Olwo lwaggulwa ku lwange?
Lwange, Lwange? - - -
Lwagulwa ku lwange?

2. Oluggi olwo lwa bonna, (A)banoonya (o)bulokozi;
Abagagg(a) era n'abaavu, Lwa buli muntu yenna. - - -

3. Tulwanyise omulabe, Oluggi lukyagguddwa,
Kkiriza ofune (e)ngule, Wesig(e) okwagala kwe. - - -

4. Fenna bwe tulisomoka, Nga tuwangudde ensi;
Ne tutikkirw(a) engule, Tunamuyimbiranga. - - -
