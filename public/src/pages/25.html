CH 162
Atikkirwe Engule
Crown Him with with Many Crowns
4
4
George J.Elvery
Doh is E 
1. Atikkirw(e) engule, Omwana gwendiga;
Muwulir(e) amatendo- ge, Agatenkanika,
Fuba mwoyo gwange, N'amaanyi go gonna;
Oyatule Kabaka wo, Eyakufiirira.

2. Atikkirw(e) engule, Eyakomererwa;
Enkovu Z'emisumaa-li, Zonna zirabika,
Teri malayika Agumiikiriza
Okulaba enkovu ze Ez'emisumaali.

3. Atikkirw(e) engule; Kabak(a) ow'eddembe!
Entalo zikome ku nsi, Tumutendereze.
Alyoke afuge, Emirembe gyonna,
Ebimuli bitimbibwe Mu nsi yaffe empya!

4. Atikkirw(e) engule, Emirembe gyonna;
Eyatonda obwengu-la, Oyo atalemwa,
Asinzibwe wonna, Eyatufiirira;
Asinzibwe, asinzibwe, Emirembe gyonna.
