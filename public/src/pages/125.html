Kabaka We Ggulu
Come, Thou Almighty King
3
4
Felice De Glardini
Doh is G 
1. Kabaka we - Ggulu, Tutenda e-rinnya lyo,
Tukusinze! Ggw omuwanguzi,
Nannyini kitiibwa, otufuge leero, Omutonzi!

2. (O)mwoyo (O)mutu-kuvu, Ggwe otuwo-lereze,
Fenna leero! Ggwe Ayinza byonna, Fuga emitima,
Totuvangako Ggwe Ow'amaanyi.

3. (O)mwana wa Ka-tonda, Okomewo- mangu nnyo;
Beera naffe! Tukole by'osiima, Byoyagala byonna
Emirembe gyonna, Tube babo.
