Mujje Eri Yesu Temulwawo
Come to the Saviour Make No Delay
4
4

Doh is Bb 
1. Mujj(e) eri Yesu temulwawo, Atudde muffe wano leero,
Fenn(a) atuyita (o)kusembera, Agamba nti "Mujje!"

Ye atwagala, atwagala, Ye atwagala abonoonyi;
Era yajj(a) okutufirira, Ffe tutuuke gy'ali.

2. Mujje abazitoowereddwa, Mwenna abakooye n'ebibi,
Mujje mwenna nnaabawummuza, Mujje mwenna gyendi.

3. Abantu bonna kiyinzika, Leero (o)kusonyiyibwa ddala!
Kub(a) ebibi by'abakkiriza, Byatwalibwa Yesu.
