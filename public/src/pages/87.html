Nga Kya Ssanyu ku Sabbiiti
How Sweet Upon this Sacred Day
3
4
G Kingaly
Doh is Eb 
1. Nga kya ssanyu ku Sa-bbiiti, (O)lunaku (o)lulonde,
Eby'ensi okubizz(a) eri, Tulowooze Yesu.

2. Kya ssany(u) okusaba leero, (E)bibi byaffe byonna,
Yesu abitusonyiwe, Olw'omusaayi gwe.

3. Ayoger(a) eby'emi-rembe, Ye Mukama mwene;
Munakuwalir(e) ebibi, Mbatwale mu ggulu.

4. Okuggibwako e-bibi, Twetaaga maanyi go;
Gwe omanyi eby'omunda, (E)kisa kyo kimala.
