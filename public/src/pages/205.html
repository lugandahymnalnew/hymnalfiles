Mumutende Yesu Omununuzi
Praise Him, Praise Him
6
8
Chester G Allen
Doh is A 
1. Mumutende Yes(u) Omununuzi waffe!
Muyimbe ku kwagala kw(e) okungi
Mumutende bamalayika mu ggulu
Erinnya lye liweebwe ekitiibwa!
Ng’Omusu-mba, anakuum(a) abaana be
Mu mikono gye abasitule.

Mumutende! Ow’ekitiibwa asinga;
Mumutende! N'ennyimb(a) ez'essanyu.

2. Mumutende Yes(u) Omununuzi waffe!
Olw’ebibi byaffe yajeezebwa
Ye lwe Lwazi, (e)ssuubi ery’obulokozi
Mumusuute eyakomererwa.
Yimba ku- Ye! Yetikk(a) ennaku zaffe
Wa kwagala (o)kung(i) okwewuunyisa,

3. Mumutende Yes(u) Omununuzi waffe!
Ab’omuggulu mumuyimbire!
Yes(u) afuga emirembe n’emirembe
Nga Kabaka atikkirwe engule!
Kristo a-jja mubuwanguzi kunsi
Obuyinza n’ekitiibwa bibye.
