CH 316
Kye Kiseera Ky'okusaba
Sweet Hour Of Prayer
An
3
8
W B Bradbury
Doh is D 
1. Kye kiseera ky'okusaba, Ka nve ku bi-gambo by'ensi;
Kang'ende eri Kitange, ntwale obu-zibu bwange.
Mu biro by'entalo zange, kampwebwe okuwummula,
Anamponya omulabe, nange ndyoke- mmusinzenga.

2. Kye kiseera ky'okusaba, Ntwala ebi-gambo byange,
Eri Oyo omwesigwa, Ansanyusa- bwenneetaaga,
Atangoba mu maaso ge, Nga nesig(a) ekigambo kye,
Ka mmukwase buli kintu, Mu kiseera- kyokusaba.

3. Kye kiseera ky'okusaba, A'ngumyenga- mu bikemo;
Ayimus(e) omwoyo gwange, Andage a-maka gange,
Bwalinnyambaz(a) ogutafa, Ngampadde ekirabo kye,
Ndiyimba n'essanyu lingi, Nga ng'enda e-yo ewaffe.
