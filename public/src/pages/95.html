NAH 333
Bye Tukuwa Biva Gy'oli
We Give Thee But Thine Own
3
4

Doh is Eb 
1. Byetukuwa biva gyoli, Kabibe nga byenkana wa!
Byetulina byonna bibyo, Birabo by'o-tuteresa.

2. (O)tuw(e) ekisa tubeereng(a) Abawanika b(o) abesigwa;
Nga twesoloza n'omwoyo, Ku bintu bye-tuli nabyo.

3. (A)mawang gonn(a) ag'omu nsi, Getaaga nny(o) Omulokozi;
Abantu be wafiirira, Bawab(a) era- basasana.

4. Gwe mulimu gwaffe fenna, (O)kukomyawo abakyama;
(O)kunoony(a) ababula era, (O)kujjanjab(a) a-bafiiriddwa.

5. Bwatyo Yesu bwe yakola, Ne yewaayo okutuwonya;
Naffe tumugoberere, Abonoonyi- balokoke.

6. Yesu yatuyigiriza, Ebyo bwe bimukolerwa;
Fenn(a) atunyikize wonna (O)kugaba n'o-kumuddiza.
