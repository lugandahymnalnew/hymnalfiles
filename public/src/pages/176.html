Mu Kibuga Kya Katonda
Shall We Gather at the River
4
4
Grant Colfax Tullar
Doh is Eb 
1. Mu kibuga kya Katonda, Okuva mu ntebe ye,-
Waliwo) omugga (o)mulungi Oguleet(a) o-bula-mu.

Tuliku’ngana ku mugga,
Ogwo gwetulisanyuki-ra-ko;
Fenna twagala- okutuu - ka
Ku mugga gw'obula-mu.

2. Yesu Omusumba waffe Anatulu’ngamyanga,-
Mu ddundiro lyokwesiima, Kumpi n'omu-gga guli.

3. Naye nga tetunnatuuka Awaal(i) omugga guli,-
Otutikkul(e) emigugu Egy'ennak(u) e-nnying(i) e-nnyo.

4. Olugendo lwaffe luno Lwa nnaku si nnyingi nnyo,-
Ffe tetulirwa kutuuka Okwetaba- ne Ye-su.
