1. Tulin(a) obubak(a) er(i) amawanga, Obunakyus(a) emitima,
Obw'amazima n’e-kisa, Bwaddembe bwa musana- -
Bwa mirembe bwa ssuubi.

Enkya ennungi ejja kunsi, Omusana gweyongere,
(O)bwakabaka bwa Yesu bujje, Obw'essanyu ne ddembe.

2. Tulin(a) oluyimb(a) er(i) amawanga, (O)lubasitul(a) emitima;
Oluwangula e-kibi, Olunagyaw(o) entalo,- -
Olunagyaw(o) entalo.

3. Tulin(a) Obubak(a) er(i) amawanga, Nti kabak(a) ow’omuggulu,
Yatuwerez(a) o-mwana we, Atulag(e) okwagala,- -
Kwagala kwa Katonda.

4. Katulag(e) ensi Omulokozi (E)yabonaabona ku lwaffe,
Abantu bonna, (a)b’o-mu nsi, Badde er(i) amazima,- -
Badde eri Katonda.
