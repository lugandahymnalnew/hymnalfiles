Tusanyuke Yesu Ajja
Joy to The World
2

Arr Lowell Mason
Doh is D 
1. Tusanyuke Yesu ajja! Ens(i) emwanirize;
Buli- o-mu-yetee-ge-ke-, N'ebitonde byo-nna
E-by'eggulu n'e-nsi, Byo-nnabimu-tendereze.

2. Yesu ajja okufuga! Fenna tuyimbe nnyo;
Enso-zi- nemigga-byo-nna-, Bimusanyuki-re,
Bi-mutendere-ze; Ka-di-yimbenga n'essanyu.

3. Yesu ajja (o)kulongosa (E)bintu byonna by'ensi!
(A)maggwa- go-nna- age-ki-bi-, Go tegalidda-yo,
O-kuzisa e-nsi, (E) ki-ko-limo kiriggwaawo.

4. Ajja (o)kuleeta eddembe! Amawanga gonna;
Gali-ra-ba- (e)kitii-bwa- kye-, Nga gatendereza,
E-ra gasanyu-ka, O-kwa-gala- kwe okungi.
