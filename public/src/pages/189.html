Olw'ekisa Kyo Nnengera
O Now I See the Crimson Wave
3
4
Mrs Joseph F Knapp
Doh is Eb 
1. Ku musalaba nnengera (E)nsuulo y’obulamu:
Mukama Omulokozi Ndab(e) ebiwundu byo,

Mu mugga gw'obulokozi Ka nnabemu ntukuzibwe;
Yesu yebal(e) antukuza, Antukuza n'omusaayi.

2. Nze nfuuka kitonde kiggya, Olw’omusaayi gwe;
Eby'edda byonna biggwawo, Olw'amaanyi ge ye.

3. Ka ntambule mu maanyi ge, Setaani tannyinze;
Ebibi byonna bigende, Yesu andokole.

4. Olw'omusaayi gwa Yesu, Ensi tekyanfuga:
Yesu Mukama yamponya, Oyo eyattibwa.
