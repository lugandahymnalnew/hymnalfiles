Nkwesiga Yesu Mukama
I am Trusting Thee
4
4
Ethlbert W Bullinger
Doh is Ab 
1. Nkwesiga Yesu Mukama, Nkwesiga wekka;
Lab(a) o-mpadd(e) obulo-kozi bwa buwa

2. Nkwesig(a) ebibi ebingi, Okunziyako;
Nkuvu-namidde Mu-kama (O)muddu wo.

3. Nkwesig(a) okunnongosanga N'omusayi gwo;
Nakwe-sigang(a) emi-sana nekiro.

4. Nkwesiga okunnu'ngamya Gye ng'enda yonna;
Buli-jjo nnaawuli-ranga Ggwe wekka.
