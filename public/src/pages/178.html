Bwe Mbeera Ku Yoludaani
On Jordan's Stormy Banks
An
4
4
T C O'kane
Doh is G 
1. Bwe mbeera ku Yoludaani, Nnengera n'essa-nyu;
Ka-naani yaffe ennungi, Mu butaka- bwa ffe.

Tuliwu-mmula mu nsi ennungi (ennungi),
Nga tuma-ze okusomo-ka--(ensi);
Tuyimb(e) o-luyimb(a) olw'Omwana (gw'Endiga),
Tubeere naye bulijjo.

2. E-mitala w'enseny(i) ezo, Butangaavu bwo-kka;
Bwe bwakabaka bwa Yesu, Agoba e-ki-ro.

3. Ne-gomba okutuukayo, Nsanyuke buli-jo;
Ntu-nulenga ku Kitaffe, Mu bwakaba-ka- bwe.

4. Nni-na essanyu mu mwoyo, Wano nze sirwee-wo,
Wa-dde ebizibu bingi, Ka ng'ume ndi-ge-nda.
