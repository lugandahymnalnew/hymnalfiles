Ndimulaba Kabaka
I Shall See the King

B D Ackley
Doh is Bb 
1. Ndimulaba Kabaka mu ggulu, Lumu nze ndimulaba
Nga ayimbirwa bamalayika Mu nsi esinga eno.

Ndimulaba, Mu kitiibwa kye
Mbe bulijjo, nga ntendereza;
Eyanfiirira, ku musalaba
Ndimulaba Ka - - - baka.

2. Mu ns(i) ey'ennyimba, Ey'ekitiibwa, Eteri kizikiza;
Ndifugir(a) eyo, wamu ne Yesu, Eyafa ku lwangenze.

3. Ndimulaba n'amaaso Kabaka, Nze ndimutendereza;
Ndiyimba ku kwagal(a) okwamussa, Ekisa kye kimala.
