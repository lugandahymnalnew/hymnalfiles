CH 218
Ku Lunaku Olw'emirembe
Hail, Peaceful Day!
An
3
4
Arr by Lowell Mason
Doh is Eb 
1. Ku lunaku (o)lw'emirembe, Ka tuyimb(e) ekitiibwa kyo;
Olutujjukiza Yesu, Lweyatonda ensi zonna,
Yalutuwa okulega, Ku ssanyu ery’omu ggulu.

2. (E)nnyimba z’okutendereza, Ziwulirw(a) eyo mu ggulu;
Ba seraafi bakusinza, Bavunnama mu maaso go,
Ensi zonna tuyimbire, Yesu eyatonda byonna.

3. (O)lunaku lulituuka ddi, Tusinze mu yekaalu yo!
Mu bwakabaka bwo Yesu, Olifuga ensi zonna,
Emirembe n'emirembe, Mu Adeni ensi empya.
