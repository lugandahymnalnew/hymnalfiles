1.Waliwo Omwoy(o) ogugamba,
"Mbuze katono (o)kukkiriza."
Naye ggw(e) omwonoonyi, leero togamba nti,
"Bwe ndifuna(a) ebbanga, ndikki - - riza.

2.Toziba matu go, munnange:
(O)mwoyo wekisa bwakuyita.
Yesu akuyita, ne bamalayika,
Omwana eyazawa, Oko - - mewo!

3.Omukisa tosubwa nate,
Oba ol(i) awo toliddayo:
(O)kuwulira Mwoyo, ng'akuyita ojje,
Ekiseera kijja, lwana - - koma.

4.Amakungula, Tegaalwewo;
Olunak(u) olw'entiisa lujja!
Ssalawo, tolemwa, Gwe tobuusabuusa;
Kkiriz(a) olwa leero, Ng'akya - - yita.
