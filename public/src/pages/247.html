SDAH 202
Katumusuute Yesu
Hail Him The King of Glory
4
4
Henry de Fluiter
Doh is Ab 
1. Tegeeza amawanga g’omu nsi, Tegeeza wonna;
Obudde bunaatera okukya, Yesu alabike.

Kabak(a) owekitiibwa: Eyattibwa ku lwaffe,
Tegeeza buli muntu, Ajj(a) okufuga.

2. (A)mawanga mangi gali mu ntalo, Entiisa nnyingi;
(O)bubonero buli wantu wonna, Bulang(a) okudda kwe.

3. Abaana be muyimbe n'essanyu, N’okujaguza;
Ba mukisa abo abalinda, Okudda kwa Yesu.
