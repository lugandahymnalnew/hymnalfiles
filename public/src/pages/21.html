CH 599
Yesu Tuzze Eri Ggwe
Father, We Come to Thee
4
4
W.J.Bostwick
Doh is F 
1. Yesu tuzz(e) eri Ggwe, Tewali mulala,
Tweyuna Ggwe wekka, Nga tusaba.
Tuli mu nzikiza, Yesu nga toliiwo,
Tuwe ku ssanyu lyo, Ayi Yesu.

Yesu tuzz(e) eri ggwe,
Tukkirize,
Tewali mulala,
Tuwulire.

2. Tuwony(e) abalabe, N'amalala g'ensi,
Tuwummule mu Ggwe, Otukuume.
Tutiisibwa mu nsi, Tuwe obuzira;
Twetaag(a) amaanyi go, Gawanguza.

3. Tuwe ku kisa kyo, Tufuule baana bo,
Otuwony(e) ebibi, Beera naffe.
Otunyweze mu Ggwe, Tuwangule ensi,
Tukwat(e) omukono, Tube naawe.
