1. Ayi Katond(a) atutuusizza, Ku lunaku olw'essanyu;
Kkiriz(a) okwebaza kwaffe, Wamu n'oku-saba kwaffe.

2. Tukugulumiza leero, Olwâ€™ennyumba eno empya;
Tulungamizenga muno, Mu kkub(o) eri-tuuka gyoli.

3. Abaazimb(a) ekkanis(a) eno, Obaweng(a) emikisa gyo,
Bonn(a) abakulu n'abato, Bamanye o-kwagala kwo.

4. Katulab(e) ekitiibwa kyo, Mu yekaalu y(o) entukuvu;
Kubang(a) obulungi bwayo, Tebugasa- nga toliimu.

5. Bwetukung'anirangamu, Netukuw(a) emyoyo gyaffe;
(O)buyinza bw'amazima go, Bwerag(e) er(a) o-tuwulire.

6. (E)kitiibwa ky(o) ekitaggwawo, Kibenga mu Kanisa yo;
Ekulember(e) abantu bo, Okubatuu-sa mu ggulu.
