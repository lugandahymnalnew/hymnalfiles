Okwagala Kwo Gwe
Love Devine

John Zundel
Doh is Bb 
1. (O)kwagala kwo Ggwe Mu-kama, Kusing(a) essanyu lyo nna;
Wekolere mu ff(e) e-weema (O)tufukek(o) ekisa-kyo.
Ggwe kwagal(a) okulo-ngofu, Yesu Ggw(e) Omusaasi-zi;
(O)bulokozi tubwe-taaga, Wony(a) obunafu bwa ffe.

2. Tussize Mwoyo wo leero, Mmalaw(o) emitawa-na
Tuyambe fenna tu-beere Abasikir(a) ebi-ngi.
Gwe Alufa ne O-mega Otuwony(e) okwonoo-na!
Tunyweze mu kukki-riza, Otutuuse mu dde-mbe.

3. Ayi Ggwe ayinza- byonna, Ekisa kyo kitu-we;
Jjangu Siir(o) obeere mu ffe Era totuvaanga-ko
Tunayimbang(a) ette-ndo lyo Tunakuweereza-nga
Nga bwe bakola mu ggulu Tunakusuutanga- Ggwe.

4. Tutonde fenna o-buggya Nga tetuliiko bba-la;
Era (o)bulokozi- bwonna (O)kutuukirira kwo-nna,
Okuyita mu ki-tiibwa Tweyongere mu maa- so,
Okutuusa lwe tu-liba Nengule mu maaso- go.
