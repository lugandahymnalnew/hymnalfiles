Njija Ku Musaalaba
I am Coming to the Cross
3
4
Win G Fischer
Doh is G 
1. Njija ku musalaba, Nze omunaku ennyo
Byonn(a) eby'ensi si kintu, Njagala bulokozi.

Nesiga Ggwe, Mukama,
Ggwe Omwana gw'Endiga
Eyafa kulw(a) ababi,
Ndokola ayi Yesu.

2. Nsaba mbeere ne Yesu, Naye siri mulungi;
Ye yekka annongoose, Kubanga yansuubiza.

3. Byonna mbiwaayo gy'oli, Emikwano ne byonna,
Omwoyo n'omubiri, Byonna bibenga bibyo.

4. Yesu bw'obeera mu nze, Mbeera mutuukirivu,
Nnongoosebwa mu byonna, Nkwebaza Ayi Yesu!
