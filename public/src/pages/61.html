Nina Mukwano Gwange
I Have a Friend So Precious
An
4
4
Hubert P Main
Doh is F 
1. Nin(a) omukwano gwange, Omulungi ennyo,
Ye Yesu anjagal(a) ennyo, Era omwesigwa:
Njagala mbeerenga naye, Ng(a)ali kumpi nange,
Mbeera wamu ne Yesu, Ngali mu nze.

2. Era bwe mba nga nkoye, Kuba ye ammanyi, 
Ampita okusembera, N'ampa amaanyi ge;
Ye antwala mu kkubo lye, (O)musana gwe gwaka,
Mbeera wamu ne Yesu, Ng'ali mu nze.

3. Mmulag(a) essanyu lyange, Awamu n'ennaku,
Mmulaga ne bye njagala, N'ebinnyiiza byonna;
Andaga ebinsaanira, A'ngamba ngezeeko,
Nnyumya wamu ne Yesu, Ng'ali mu nze.

4. Amanyi nga njagala, (O)kubuulir(a) ejiri,
Andaga bwe mba njogera Ebyo ebiyamba;
Eby'ekisa kye ekingi (E)kyamuleet(a) okufa:
Tukola fembi Yesu, Ng'ali nange.
