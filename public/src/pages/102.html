Yesu Ali Ku Luggi
There's a Stranger at the Door
An
4

George C Stebbins
Doh is Db 
1. Yesu ali ku luggi,
Ggulawo; (Ayimiridde x2)
Akulindiridde nnyo,
Ggula-wo, (Ayimiridde x2)
Muyingize tagenda,
Ye Mutukuvu Yesu,
Omwana wa Katonda,
Ggula-wo. (Ayimiridde x2)

2. Wulira akuyita,
Wulira; (Eddoboozi lye x2)
Londa ye Vva ku by'ensi,
Wuli-ra; (Eddoboozi lye x2)
Ali awo ku luggi,
Anakuwa essanyu;
Olw'erinya ly(e) egganzi,
Wuli-ra. (Eddoboozi lye x2)

3. Ggulawo (o)mutima gwo,
Ggulawo; (Yesu ayingire x2)
Bw'onolwa (a)nakuvaako,
Ggula-wo. (Yesu ayingire x2)
Ye mukwano gwo ddala,
(O)mutima gw(o) agutaasa,
So takwabulirenga, 
Ggula-wo. (Yesu ayingire x2)

4. Yesu ava mu ggulu,
Ggulawo; (Yesu ayingire x2)
Alye awamu naawe,
Ggula-wo, (Yesu ayingire x2)
Alyok(e) akulokole,
Kkiriz(a) okwagala kwe,
Akutwale mu ggulu,
Ggula-wo. (Yesu ayingire x2)
