Olijja Ddi Mukama?
How Long, O Lord our Savior?
An
4
4
George J Webb
Doh is Bb 
1. Olijja ddi Mukama (O)mulokozi waffe?
Fenna tukwetaaga nnyo Olijja ddi Yesu?
Olunaku lwo lujje Olwekitalo ennyo;-
Ekitiibwa kyo kyonna Kikke ku baana bo.

2. Tukwetaaga ggwe Yesu Ojje mu bantu bo,
Tulowooza ng(a) oludde Abamu bakooye,
Bakola eby'ekyejo N'abamu ku babo,-
Beetegese batono Okukwaniriza.

3. Mukama (o)tuzukuse Otukubirize
Tubuulire bonna nti Yesu ojja mangu.
(E)ttabaza zaffe zaake Twesib(e) amazima,-
Era tweteeketeeke Okukwaniriza.
